About Dr. Dibya Ranjan Behera

Dr. Dibya Ranjan Behera is a renowned Obstetrician and Gynecologist with over 8 years of experience. He has completed his MBBS, DGO, and DNB in Obstetrics and Gynecology. He is known for his expertise in managing high-risk pregnancies, infertility treatments, and laparoscopic surgeries.

Facilities Provided

  • High-risk pregnancy management
  • Infertility treatments
  • Laparoscopic surgeries
  • Comprehensive obstetric care
  • Gynecological health screenings
